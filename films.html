Passion Film is an industry leader in advertising and films with over 100 ad films to their credit. The first Indian production house to bag a Cannes Dolphin Award and Gold Stevie Award, Passion Film has been pivotal in putting the Indian ad film industry on the map. They have gained a reputation as a highly professional production house providing end to end solutions. The approach is a perfect combination of creativity and technical acumen to bring the vision of every client to life.

Passion Film has worked with stellar names in the Bollywood industry like Shah Rukh Khan, Salman Khan, Akshay Kumar, Alia Bhatt, Ranveer Singh, Kareena Kapoor, Katrina Kaif, Jhanvi Kapoor and Sonu Sood among several others. With a commitment to create visual masterpieces and an in-depth understanding of the demands of the advertising industry, Passion Film is the go-to production house for brands and agencies.
